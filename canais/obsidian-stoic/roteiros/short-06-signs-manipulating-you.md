### [HOOK — 0 to 8s]

Have you ever walked away from a conversation feeling drained and
confused — everything somehow your fault, but you can't figure out
how? You start wondering, "Am I being too sensitive?" What if that
confusion isn't an accident? What if it's a strategy?

> in bone white.
> [3-6s] Close-up of hands fidgeting in a dark room. Shadows moving
> across the wall. Text: "AM I TOO SENSITIVE?" in bone white, italic.
> [6-8s] Sharp cut to obsidian black. Text: "WHAT IF IT'S A STRATEGY?"
> in ember gold. Bass hit.

### [SECTION 1 — 8 to 25s]

First: **they rewrite history**. They deny what they said, twist your
words, and slowly replace your reality with theirs.

Second: **guilt becomes currency**. Their mood is suddenly your
responsibility, and you'll bend yourself in half trying to fix
something you didn't break.

Third: **the silent treatment**. They punish you with absence — texts
ignored, presence erased — until you're desperate enough to apologize
for nothing.

> VISUAL: [8-14s] Cracked mirror in a dark room, fractured reflection.
> Text: "**THEY REWRITE HISTORY**" in ember gold. Slow pan.
> [14-19s] Chains wrapped around open hands, ember gold light on
> the links. Text: "**GUILT BECOMES CURRENCY**" in ember gold.
> [19-25s] Phone screen showing "Read" with no reply. Dark room.
> Text: "**SILENT TREATMENT**" in ember gold. Whoosh.

---

### [SECTION 2 — 25 to 45s]

Fourth: **they flood then vanish**. One day you're everything to them.
The next, you don't exist. That emotional whiplash keeps you addicted
and chasing.

Fifth: **hidden aggression**. Sarcastic jokes, backhanded compliments,
conveniently "forgotten" promises. Their anger never comes out clean
— it leaks through cracks they'll never admit exist.

> VISUAL: [25-31s] Split screen — left side shows warm embrace, right
> side shows an empty chair. Ember gold vs cold blue. Text: "**FLOOD
> THEN VANISH**" in ember gold.
> [31-38s] Close-up of a smiling mouth that slowly fades to a clenched
> jaw. High contrast. Text: "**HIDDEN AGGRESSION**" in ember gold.
> [38-45s] All 5 signs flash on screen: REWRITE HISTORY → GUILT →
> SILENCE → FLOOD/VANISH → HIDDEN AGGRESSION. 1s each. Hold on all 5.
