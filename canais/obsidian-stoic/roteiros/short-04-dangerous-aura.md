energy shifts? Nobody greets them with a joke. Nobody tests them.

back. That's not confidence. That's a dangerous aura — and it's
built, not born.

### [BODY — 8 to 45s]

It starts with **controlled silence**. Most people fill every gap
with nervous words. A dangerous presence speaks only when the
silence becomes more uncomfortable than anything they could say.

Next is **unpredictability**. When people can't read your next move,
they can't control you. Predictable people get managed. Unpredictable
ones get feared.

Then there's **emotional detachment** — staying completely unmoved
when everything around you is chaos. Someone insults you and you
don't flinch. That's not acting. That's power.

And finally, **strategic eye contact**. Most people look away first.
You don't. Not aggressively — calmly. As if you already know the
outcome.

> VISUAL: [8-14s] Man sitting alone at a dark table, hands clasped,
> utterly still. Others talking around him. Text: "**CONTROLLED
> SILENCE**" in ember gold. Slow zoom in.
> [14-20s] Chess board mid-game, a hand hovering over a piece but not
> moving. Ember gold light. Text: "**UNPREDICTABILITY**" in ember
> gold. Whoosh transition.
> [20-28s] Close-up of a face completely unmoved while wind and rain
> hit it. High contrast. Text: "**EMOTIONAL DETACHMENT**" in ember
> gold. Hold.
> [28-35s] Extreme close-up of eyes, steady and unblinking. Ember
> gold light on the iris. Text: "**STRATEGIC EYE CONTACT**" in ember
> gold. Slow zoom. Bass hit on "outcome."
> [35-45s] All 4 labels flash on screen in sequence (1.5s each):
> SILENCE → UNPREDICTABILITY → DETACHMENT → EYE CONTACT.
