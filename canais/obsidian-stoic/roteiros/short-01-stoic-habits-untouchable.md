### [INTRO & HOOK — 0 to 8s]

He used to be a victim of his emotions — thrown off by every setback,
every insult, every person who didn't deserve that power over him.
Now, he walks through chaos with a calm that can't be broken. What
changed? It's not willpower. It's not motivation. It's 7 ancient
habits that built an unbreakable fortress inside him.

### [SECTION 1 — 8 to 45s]

And let's be clear: this isn't about suppressing what you feel. It's
about turning raw emotion into something most people never develop —
real control. You'll learn to build that sacred pause between what
happens and how you react, finally understanding that your peace of
mind is never for sale. Not to anyone. You'll also learn to ask
yourself the one question that destroys anxiety on contact: "Will
this even matter in a year?" That single question strips the panic.
It exposes what's actually worth your energy and what's just noise
trying to own you. This is how you stop being a puppet and start
commanding your own mind.

> VISUAL: [8-12s] Wide shot of Greek temple ruins at twilight, slow
> pan right. Atmospheric fog. Text overlay in bone white: "NOT ABOUT
> SUPPRESSING" fades to "REAL CONTROL" in ember gold.
> [12-18s] Close-up of marble hands in prayer position, dramatic side
> lighting. Slow zoom in. Text overlay: "SACRED PAUSE" in ember gold,
> center screen. Subtle whoosh transition.
> [18-25s] Rain hitting dark stone surface in slow motion. Reflections
> of distant lights in puddles. Text overlay: "WILL THIS EVEN MATTER
> IN A YEAR?" in bone white, appearing word by word.
> [25-32s] Lone figure on a rooftop at night, city lights below, wind
> moving their coat. Silhouette, backlit. Text: "JUST NOISE" fades to
> nothing. Pan up slowly.
> [32-38s] Close-up of a chess king standing alone, other pieces
> toppled around it. Ember gold side lighting. Slow zoom out.
> [38-45s] Extreme close-up of marble statue eyes, side-lit with sharp
