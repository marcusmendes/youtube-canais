Ever walked away from a conversation feeling confused — only to
realize you apologized for something you didn't even do? That wasn't

powerful they should be illegal — and someone is using them on you
right now.

> [3-5s] Cut to close-up of puppet strings against black background,

> blood red. Slow zoom in. Text: "5 TRICKS THAT SHOULD BE ILLEGAL"

### [BODY — 8 to 45s]

It starts with **love bombing**. They flood you with affection until
you're addicted, then pull it all away and watch you beg for it back.

Next comes **gaslighting** — they twist your words and deny your
reality until you trust their version more than your own memory.

Third, **mirroring**. They copy your body language to build a
connection that was never real, slipping past every guard you have.

Then there's the **foot-in-the-door** — one small yes today turns
into complete compliance tomorrow.

And finally, **guilt-tripping**. They weaponize your own kindness
until your compassion becomes a chain you can't break.

> VISUAL: [8-14s] Dark silhouette couple, one embracing the other
> from behind — possessive, not loving. Side-lit ember gold. Text
> overlay: "LOVE BOMBING" in ember gold, center screen. Slow zoom out.
> [14-20s] Cracked mirror in a dark room, fractured reflection of a
> face. Cold lighting. Text: "GASLIGHTING" in ember gold. Pan across
> the cracks slowly. Whoosh on transition.
> [20-26s] Two identical shadowed figures facing each other like a
> mirror. One side-lit, one in darkness. Text: "MIRRORING" in ember
> gold. Static hold with subtle zoom.
> [26-32s] Close-up of a hand reaching for a chess piece (pawn) on
> a dark board. Ember gold side lighting. Text: "FOOT-IN-THE-DOOR"
> in ember gold. Slow pull back to reveal more pieces.
> [32-38s] Chains draped across open hands, ember gold light on the
> links against obsidian black. Text: "GUILT-TRIPPING" in ember gold.
> Slow zoom in on the chains. Bass hit on "break."
> [38-45s] All 5 terms flash on screen in sequence (1 second each),
> ember gold on black: LOVE BOMBING → GASLIGHTING → MIRRORING →
> FOOT-IN-THE-DOOR → GUILT-TRIPPING. Final hold on all 5 stacked.
