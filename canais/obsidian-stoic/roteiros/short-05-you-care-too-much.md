## SCRIPT

### [INTRO & HOOK — 0 to 8s]

What if your constant need for approval isn't a weakness you can just
"fix"? You've heard the same advice a thousand times: "Just stop
caring what people think." But it doesn't work, does it? You're
biologically wired to seek validation — it's a survival program. But
what if you could rewrite it?

> VISUAL: [0-3s] Close-up of a person's eyes darting nervously
> between unseen people. Shallow depth of field. Dark tones. Text:
> "NEED FOR APPROVAL" in bone white.
> [3-5s] Cut to brain scan imagery stylized in ember gold on black.
> Slow rotation. Text: "SURVIVAL PROGRAM" in ember gold.
> [5-8s] Glitch transition to a marble statue face, cracked, golden
> light seeping through cracks. Text: "REWRITE IT" in ember gold.
> Bass hit.

---

### [SECTION 1 — 8 to 25s]

This creates a painful loop: the more you care, the more you lose
yourself. That need becomes an invisible energy drain — like an app
running in the background of your mind, forcing you to perform for
people who aren't even watching. Sound familiar?

> VISUAL: [8-14s] Figure in a crowd, everyone blurred and moving
> fast, figure frozen in place. Slow zoom out. Text: "ENERGY DRAIN"
> in ember gold.
> [14-20s] Phone screen showing notifications piling up, reflected
> on a face in darkness. Text: "PERFORMING FOR NO ONE" in bone white.
> [20-25s] Empty theater seats, spotlight on an empty stage. Slow
> pan across. Hold.

---

### [SECTION 2 — 25 to 45s]

The secret isn't becoming cold. It's **conscious detachment** —
caring wisely instead of desperately. The real mind control trick
is flipping one question. Stop asking "How do I get them to like
me?" and start asking "Do I even respect this person's opinion?"
That single shift changes everything.

> VISUAL: [25-30s] Split screen — one side shows a puppet on strings,
> other side shows hands cutting the strings with a blade. Ember gold
> light on the blade. Text: "**CONSCIOUS DETACHMENT**" in ember gold.
> [30-37s] Close-up of a face transitioning from anxious to calm.
> Side-lit. Text fades in word by word: "DO I RESPECT THIS OPINION?"
> in bone white.
> [37-45s] Lone figure standing tall, backlit by golden light, others
> as shadows below. Text: "EVERYTHING CHANGES" in ember gold. Bass
> hit. Hold 2 seconds.

---

### [CTA & CONCLUSION — 45 to 55s]

Subscribe to keep rewriting your internal code. What's the one opinion
you'll stop chasing today? Comment below.

> VISUAL: [45-50s] Obsidian black. Text: "REWRITE YOUR CODE" in ember
> gold, center screen.
> [50-55s] Final frame: "WHAT WILL YOU STOP CHASING?" in bone white.
> Hold 3 seconds. Fade to black.
