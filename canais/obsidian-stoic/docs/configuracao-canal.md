## 3. Visual Identity

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary** | Obsidian Black | `#0A0A0A` | Background, dominant tone |
| **Secondary** | Ash Gray | `#2A2A2A` | Subtle gradients, overlays |
| **Accent** | Ember Gold | `#C8963E` | Text highlights, key phrases, logo accent |
| **Text** | Bone White | `#F0EDE5` | Main text, subtitles |
| **Alert** | Blood Red | `#8B1A1A` | Occasional emphasis (danger, warning topics) |

### Typography Style

- **Primary font feel:** Bold, condensed sans-serif (similar to Bebas Neue,
  Anton, or Oswald). All-caps for key phrases.
- **Secondary font feel:** Clean sans-serif (similar to Inter, Montserrat)
  for body text/subtitles.

### Overall Aesthetic

Dark, cinematic, minimal. Think: marble statues in shadow, lone figures
in urban landscapes at night, Greek architecture fragments, cracked
stone textures. Never cartoonish, never bright. The mood is quiet
intensity — not aggressive, but unsettling and contemplative.
